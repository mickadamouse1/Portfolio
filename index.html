MY STORY

My name is Michael Chambers and I am a student at University studying Computer Science. I am currently self-studying Software Development and Web Design. I hope this blog gives you some insight into who I am and what I'm about.

So, about me, uhm. I've always had a dream to travel the world and venture to every corner of this planet. But what good is there in seeing all these places without being able to share it with others? exactly. Therefore, I decided to start looking into creating blogs. Then after venturing into a deep rabit hole on the big ol' internet, I ran into a topic about how web developers create websites as their personal blogs, rather than using a premade blogging website.

I immediately thought this was really cool.

That's when I decided that I'd do more research on the topic and eventually find myself in love with the idea of being a software developer. I had always wanted to be a programmer after all. Except I was the kind of kid who wanted to be some sort of dark-ops elite hacker.

I still kinda want to be a dark-ops hacker... oopse.
